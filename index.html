About me

Welcome to my personal homepage, and thank you for dropping by. My name is Henrik, I am 22 years old, and currently study web development at NTNU Gjøvik. My hometown is Lillehammer, which is approximately 45 minutes away from my university. It is known for hosting the winter Olympics in 1994. Before I moved into a dorm here in Gjøvik last year, I lived there with my family consisting of my parents and my two younger brothers. I make sure to visit them every weekend, especially as I also have a job at the local Joker store.

Whenever I have some time to spare I enjoy relaxing with a good Nintendo game. In particular, The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild is one of my favourites. One quote from the game I would like to share is from the opening scene where the main character, Link, hears princess Zelda saying: Link... You are the light. OUR light... that must shine upon Hyrule once again. Now go.
